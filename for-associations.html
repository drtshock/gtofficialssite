Simple Process

How It Works

One point of contact. Zero headaches. Here's the process from start to finish.

Send Us Your Schedule

Give us your game schedule — regular season, tournaments, or both. Tell us the sports, age levels, and locations. That's all we need to get started.

We Handle the Rest

We recruit and assign qualified officials to every game on your schedule using Arbiter's professional scheduling platform. If someone cancels, we find a replacement.

One Invoice, One Contact

You pay GT Officials. We handle all official payouts individually. No tracking dozens of 1099 workers. No chasing people down. One clean transaction.

Why Partner With Us

Your Games Deserve Reliable Officials

When an official doesn't show up, your program looks bad — not the assignor. That's why we take reliability personally. GT Officials was built on the principle that associations deserve a partner they can count on, game after game.

Deep Roster

We maintain a large roster of trained officials across multiple sports, which means we have backup plans for backup plans.

Professional Scheduling

We use Arbiter — the industry standard for sports scheduling — to manage every assignment with full transparency and accountability.

Multi-Sport Coverage

Baseball, basketball, softball, and football. Whether you need officials for one sport or several, we've got you covered under one partnership.

Responsive Communication

When you call or email GT Officials, you'll hear back quickly. Schedule changes, last-minute additions, questions about coverage — we're on it.

Tournaments & Regular Season

We handle both. From a weekend tournament with 30 games to a full regular season schedule, we scale to fit your needs.
