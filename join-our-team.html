The GT Difference

Why Officials Choose Us

We've heard the frustrations. Here's how we do things differently.

Fastest Payouts Around

This is our signature. We don't sit on your money for weeks. GT Officials processes payouts quickly after games so you get paid when you should — not when someone finally gets around to it.

We Actually Respond

Call, text, or email — we get back to you. No more wondering if your assignor got your message. We know your time matters and we treat your questions with the same urgency you'd expect from any professional relationship.

Organized Scheduling

All assignments managed through Arbiter — the industry standard. Accept or decline games from your phone, see your schedule clearly, and never wonder whether you're confirmed.

Volume & Variety

We work with associations across the Twin Cities metro — baseball, basketball, softball, and more. That means more games available and more opportunities to fill your calendar during peak season.

No Exclusivity Required

You can work with GT Officials and keep your current assignor. We're not asking you to burn any bridges — just add more games to your schedule with an assignor who treats you right. Many of our officials work with multiple associations.
