About GT Officials

Built on Reliability. Run with Care.

GT Officials was founded by Gail Hensler to solve a simple problem: leagues and tournaments deserve officials who show up, and officials deserve an assignor who pays on time and communicates clearly.

Serving the Twin Cities metro area, GT Officials handles the full process — from recruiting and training officials to scheduling and paying them — so associations can focus on running their programs.
